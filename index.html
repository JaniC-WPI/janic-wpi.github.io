Hi there, this is Jani!

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Robotics Engineering at the MER Lab in  Worcester Polytechnic Institute (graduating summer 2025), where I design and develop deep learning-driven algorithms for vision-based control and motion planning in autonomous robotic systems. My research aims to eliminate dependencies on external markers and reduce reliance on predefined robot models, camera parameters, or onboard proprioceptive sensors.

Focusing on the intersection of Robotics, Computer Vision, Deep Learning and AI, my interest lies in developing affordable and accessible robots that can be easily controlled using a mobile camera, with a particular focus on applications that positively impact and improve the environment.
